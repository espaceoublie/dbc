About HydroLIFE

Our mission is to promote professional standards in hydrotherapy and connect clients with qualified practitioners across Indonesia.

Professional Hydrotherapy Network

HydroLIFE was founded to establish a network of certified hydrotherapists who meet rigorous professional standards. Our certification program ensures all members have the necessary skills and knowledge to provide safe, effective aquatic therapy.

We offer three levels of certification: Certified Hydrotherapist (C.HydroT), Certified Advanced Hydrotherapist (CA.HydroT), and Certified Master Hydrotherapist (CM.HydroT). Each level requires specific training and demonstrated competency.

Our Services

Our Services

Hydrotherapy offers numerous benefits for various conditions. Our certified therapists provide specialized treatments tailored to individual needs.

Neurosensory Hydrotherapy

Specialized aquatic therapy for neurological conditions, sensory processing disorders, and brain injuries using water's unique properties to stimulate neural pathways.

Pediatric Hydrotherapy

Therapeutic interventions for children with special needs, developmental delays, or physical disabilities in a fun, supportive aquatic environment.

Musculoskeletal Rehabilitation

Water-based exercises and treatments for injury recovery, arthritis, chronic pain, and post-surgical rehabilitation with reduced joint stress.

Aquatic Fitness

Low-impact fitness programs for all ages and fitness levels, including prenatal water exercise and senior aquatic fitness classes.
